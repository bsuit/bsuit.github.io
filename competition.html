What is the bSUIT Coding Competition?



The bSUIT Coding Contest, held by bSUIT every quarter, is a global coding competition where programmers can gain coding experience regardless of their background. Contestants will be constantly challenged with algorithmic problems around the clock. For highly ranked participants, bSUIT will provide networking opportunities to employers of 500+ tech companies.

Each problem brings new types of challenges to the contestant and everyone will have the chance to put their algorithmic skills to the test!

How does it work?



After registering for the coding contest, make a codeforces account. Instructions will be sent through email after registration. Afterwards, on May 30th 1:00PM PST, a link will be sent to you via email. High ranking contestants will be announced on June 1st, 2020.

There will be five problems given in the span of 2 hours. Your score and rank will be automatically updated as you submit. Your performance will be graded by
1) the total time you spent 2) accuracy of your answers 3) difficulty of the problem you've solved.
